Game Rules

  • Each player can move 3 spaces horizontally or vertically.
  • You can't pass obstacles
  • To Increase Your Power Take Weapons, there are 4 weapons
  • Weapons power will decrease the other player health when the battle starts
  • each player has a health of 100
  • The current and the default weapon has a power of 10

  • If players cross over adjacent squares (horizontally or vertically), a battle begins.
  • You can choose to attack or to defend
  • If you choose to attack the damage will depend on the weapon power
  • If the player chooses to defend themselves, they sustain 50% less damage than normal
